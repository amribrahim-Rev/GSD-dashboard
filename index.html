Why email failed
Leads use Outlook / Microsoft 365. Cold emails from Google domains landed in spam.
Fix applied: added 5 Outlook domains. 22 mailboxes total, 10 Outlook.

Learnings

✅ The opener works. "Do you handle steel connections?" pulled a 45% reply rate, 2.6x above the 17% HQ benchmark. Keep it.
⚠️ No locked Message 2. After a "yes," there was no set follow-up, so threads drifted or died on Eid greetings with no CTA. Biggest fix for the first two weeks.
❌ Email deliverability, not copy, was the problem. Zero replies came from Google domains hitting Outlook spam folders. Fixed with Outlook mailboxes.
📌 HeyReach tags are unreliable. 3 of 8 "Interested" tags were just Eid greetings. Use real message content as ground truth.
🔌 Hichem's account disconnected. 5 warm leads need manual LinkedIn follow-up.
